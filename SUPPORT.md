## FAQ

**How do I start a focus session?**
Click the timer icon in your menu bar, select a duration, and press Start.

**How do I change the timer duration?**
Select any preset duration (5, 15, 25, 45, or 60 minutes) or add a custom duration in Settings → Timer.

**How do I change the theme?**
Go to Settings → Theme and choose from 10 visual themes.

**How do I change the language?**
Go to Settings → General → Language. Note: requires app restart to apply.

**Why don't I hear a sound when the timer ends?**
Make sure notifications are enabled in Settings → Sound, and that FocusBar has notification permission in System Settings → Notifications.

**Does FocusBar collect my data?**
No. FocusBar stores all data locally on your Mac. No data is sent to any server. See our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY_POLICY.md) for details.

**Does FocusBar require an internet connection?**
No. FocusBar works entirely offline with no internet connection required.
