# FocusBar — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 25, 2026**

## Overview

FocusBar is a productivity timer app for macOS. We believe your data belongs to you. This policy explains what data FocusBar collects and how it's handled.

## Data Collection

**FocusBar does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

Specifically:

- **No analytics** — We do not track usage, sessions, or behavior
- **No crash reporting** — No crash data is sent to any server
- **No advertising** — No ads, no ad networks, no tracking
- **No network access** — The app makes zero internet connections

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your Mac using macOS UserDefaults:

- Timer state (current session progress)
- Settings preferences (theme, duration, language, etc.)
- Session statistics (sessions completed, minutes focused, streaks)
- Custom tags and durations

This data never leaves your device. It is not synced, backed up to any cloud service, or accessible to anyone but you.

## Notifications

FocusBar uses macOS User Notifications to alert you when sessions end. Notification permissions are requested on first launch. You can disable notifications at any time in the app's Settings or in System Settings → Notifications.

## Third-Party Services

FocusBar uses no third-party services, frameworks, or SDKs that collect data.

## Children

FocusBar is suitable for all ages and is rated 4+ on the App Store. No data is collected from anyone, including children.

## Changes

If this policy changes, we will update it here with a revised date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
